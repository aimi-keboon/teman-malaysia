Teman Malaysia provides the best care companion for your loved ones.

We provide care companionship services to improve your loved one's quality of life and provide needed assistance for them.

How it works?

Customers will be matched with Temanions to perform daily tasks, run errands, attend health appointments, or simply enjoy their free time through planned customized activities.

Who are Temanions?

Temanions are passionate and caring Malaysians who also are care companions. The term is a play of words between the Malay word "teman" and the English word "companion".

Our Vision

  • To be the leading care companion services provider in Malaysia.

Our Mission

  • To utilise technology in the care companionship services.

  • To amplify awareness on the importance of ageing gracefully by informative content on our website and social media.

  • To provide job opportunities that help to contribute to decreasing the unemployment rate in Malaysia.
