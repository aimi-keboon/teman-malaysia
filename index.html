TEMAN

We provide care companionship services to improve your loved one's quality of life and provide needed assistance for them.

Teman Malaysia offers a safe experience by providing guardians' updates on their loved ones' whereabouts and activities.

We will match our customers with our Temanions to perform varied activities such as daily chores, attend appointments, or enjoy their free time through a library of curated activities.

Our Service

Our Promise

We are committed to providing the best care possible for your loved ones.

We can't always be there for our loved ones. If we are travelling, working long hours, or frequently unavailable due to pressing obligations, it might not always be possible to provide the care and attention they need.

Their needs must be met as well, even if this means finding trusted companions or caregivers outside the family circle who can fill in when needed.

Teman Malaysia strives to provide the best service for customers by providing companionship and caregiving services for the elderly, pregnant women, mothers with small child and differently abled people.

Our Temanions or caregivers are carefully selected as we want you to have peace of mind knowing your loved ones will be in reliable and trusted hands.
